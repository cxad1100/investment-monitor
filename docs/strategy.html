What’s honest here. The universe is survivorship-corrected — 192 EUR names that collapsed/delisted 2018→now are included and liquidated by the graveyard at their last price — and this config is survivorship-clean — across the whole backtest it held 0 names that later died, so its return is not inflated by dead winners vanishing. The real remaining caveat is capacity: a concentrated top-10 of German small/mid-caps can post outsized momentum returns that are hard to realise at size (slippage is modeled, not measured). Also: daily closes only, €1/order costs assumed, and past performance is not future returns. Validation (out-of-sample, 2023→) is the guard against curve-fitting.
